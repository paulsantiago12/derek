Abstract The present invention is the next step towards making glasses more interesting for the people who lack clear eye sight. The spectra would be known for its self adjusting spectacle glasses in which a camera has the auto focusing capacity, wherein spectra can adjust their glasses according to the person's eyesight number. The person does not need to visit the ophthalmologist periodically now. Not only will these glasses have the ability to do this but it can also contain three other functions; (i) camera, (ii) using it as sunglasses and (iii) the binocular feature.
Description FIELD OF THE INVENTION The present invention relates about self adjusting spectacle glasses. The invention is more particularly used towards making glasses more interesting for the people who lack clear eye sight. The invention is a step up for the current spectacles, even better lenses. It is an improvement towards eye products , which is beneficial for daily needs.
